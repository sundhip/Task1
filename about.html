OrganXcell

AI-Based Organ Matching and Transplant Management System

Health-Tech AI

Problem: Traditional clinical organ matching queues are slow, complex, and prone to manual scheduling bottlenecks that increase organ expiration risks.

Solution: Developed an AI-assisted compatibility prediction and management platform. The system scores donor-recipient pairs utilizing key biological parameters and manages emergency routing database lists.

Key Features:

  • Compatibility Scoring: ML match rating calculations based on HLA markers and blood compatibility.
  • Priority Allocation: Auto-factors severity of case, age variables, and geographical distance weights.
  • Recipient Ranking: Generates ordered priority queues.
  • Organ Database: Full CRUD portal to manage active donors and recipients.

Technologies: Python, Django, MySQL, Machine Learning.
